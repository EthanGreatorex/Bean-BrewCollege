Baking Lessons

Choose Your Baking Lesson

Choose from a variety of baking lessons.

Beginner Baking Lessons

Perfect for newcomers, these classes cover the fundamentals of baking, measuring ingredients, understanding dough and batter textures, and mastering simple recipes like cookies, muffins, and cupcakes.

Bread Baking Lessons

Dive into the art of breadmaking — from rustic sourdoughs to soft sandwich loaves. Students learn about yeast fermentation, kneading, proofing, and baking techniques for different types of bread.

Cake Baking Lessons

These lessons focus on the creative side of baking — frosting, piping, fondant work, and layered cake design. Students practice professional decorating techniques for events and celebrations.

Seasonal-themed Baking Classes

Perfect for newcomers, these classes cover the fundamentals of baking, measuring ingredients, understanding dough and batter textures, and mastering simple recipes like cookies, muffins, and cupcakes.

Family Baking Lessons

Fun, hands-on workshops centered around holidays, seasons, or special events — think Christmas cookies, Valentine’s desserts, or summer fruit tarts.

Professional Baking Lessons

Interactive sessions where children (and sometimes parents) learn simple baking skills while making fun treats. The main focus of the lesson is on fun, safety, and creativity.

Pastry Baking Lessons

A deep dive into French-style pastries such as croissants, danishes, éclairs, and tarts. Emphasis is placed on precision, lamination, and professional presentation.

Dietary Baking Lessons

Lessons focusing on specific baking niches such as gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, or allergen-friendly recipes.
